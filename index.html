PEEK-The Social Media Dashboard





















About Peek

Peek is a user-friendly social media dashboard. After logging in, you select your desired social media and the account you want to look at. This will lead you to a page that will show you the basic data of followers, views etc. along with the latest posts.


Why choose Peek?

The desire to spend more time on social media is increasing day by day. Often people open there accounts to look at one thing but end up wasting hours going through random posts. Peek helps you to regulate and get out of the habit of swiping through social media for hours. Apart from that we will continue to improve and work on updates to make peek applicable to a wider group of audience and for customer satisfaction.
